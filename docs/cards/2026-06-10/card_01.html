Scotland's Remote Island Records
Highest PFAS Levels in Drinking Water
Key Finding
A remote island in Scotland has been identified as having the highest PFAS contamination levels in drinking water across the country. Despite geographic isolation, "forever chemicals" have permeated the local water supply — raising urgent questions about contamination pathways and regulatory oversight in rural communities.
